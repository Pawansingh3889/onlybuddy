## BUDGET SPLIT RECOMMENDATION (£150–£300/month)
## ──────────────────────────────────────────

| Platform          | Monthly Budget | Why                                               |
|-------------------|---------------|---------------------------------------------------|
| Facebook/Instagram| £120–£160     | Best targeting for 25–45 age group in Hull        |
| TikTok            | £20–£80       | Best for 18–28, organic-feeling, cheaper CPM      |
| Snapchat          | £10–£60       | Cheapest reach, good for under-25s                |

**Targeting settings for all platforms:**
- Location: Hull, East Yorkshire — 10 mile radius
- Age: 18–45
- Interests: flexible work, side hustle, gig economy, local community
- Exclude: people already in delivery/courier jobs (reduces wasted spend)

**Most important thing to do BEFORE spending a penny on ads:**
1. Install your Meta/TikTok/Snapchat pixels in JoinPage.js (the pixel IDs)
2. Set up a "Lead" conversion event in each ad platform
3. Only optimise for conversions AFTER you have 10+ leads — before that, optimise for "Landing page views"
